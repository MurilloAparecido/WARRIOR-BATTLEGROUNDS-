WARRIOR BATTLEGROUNDS

story: the story of the game begins in china when ninjas were hired by the feudal lord to kill a traveler who came from japan, 3 days later 3 ninjas ambushed him and killed him without him being able to defend himself, little did the ninjas know that they had killed the second commander of the samurai, after having one of their commander killed, the samurai are outraged and leave for china in search of revenge.

HOW TO PLAY?

how to play? samurai battlegrounds is a card game in which you depend on strategy and game sense to win, each team has 8 cards, the game works by eliminating cards, if your card has the three attributes better than the other card you play the other card to the dead zone, if your card is only 2 attributes superior to another card you leave the card inactive for the next two moves, the game also has a neutralization system where if you eliminate 3 cards in a row from your enemy you get a bonus and eliminates 3 more, if you neutralize 3 cards in a row from your enemy you get a bonus and eliminate two more cards from him.
